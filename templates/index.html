AutoNote

Automatically take notes on pieces of text

Enter the text that you want to take notes on below.

IMPORTANT: Make sure that each paragraph of your text is on ONE LINE. Spaces after periods in a sentence are fine, but don't put spaces after periods anywhere else.

Use >> to create headings in your notes. For example, >>Section A will show as

Section A

The program takes notes on important dates, numerical figures, and quotes. It also picks out the most relevant details from the text.
